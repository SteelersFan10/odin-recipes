Penne Alla Vodka (Trent's Favorite)

Penne Pasta Bathed In A Creamy, Tomatoe-y Pink Vodka Sauce (Don't Forget The Garlic Bread)

This is my childhood favorite. Penne Rigate cooked al dente, served in a 'pink' sauce made from reduced tomatoe sauce with a splash of heavy cream and vodka. This pairs well with grilled or breaded chicken and some Italian bread, especially in America where we eat, and do everything, in excess!

Check out this recipe by Vincenzo's Plate, here.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Penne Rigate
  • 1 can whole tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 yellow onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 3 tbsp E.V.O.O.
  • Splash of vodka
  • Sun dried tomoatoes (optional)
  • Bacon or proscuitto (optional)
  • Chicken breast (optional)
  • Italian bread (optional)

Step by Step

  1. Heat pan to medium-low
  2. Dice onion and add to pan with olive oil and garlic
  3. Crush tomatoes with hands as onions simmer for 5 mins
  4. Add splash of vodka and let ethanol cook out
  5. Add newly crushed tomato sauce to pan and reduce for 15-20 mins
  6. While sauce is cooking, add pasta to pot and cook for 12 mins or al dente
  7. When tomatoe sauce is reduced to non-watery consistency, add half cup of heavy cream. Stir and check color. Add more if necessary.
  8. Add in basil leaves and sun dried tomatoes, along with strained pasta
  9. Stir and use wrist to mix creamy sauce and pasta. Serve.
